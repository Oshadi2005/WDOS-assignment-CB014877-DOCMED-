THE SERVICES WE OFFER

Our Services

Our modern facilities handle all of your healthcare needs, both preventive and curative. All together, our facilities have space for 190 in-house patients, so you can be sure that the treatment they receive meets or exceeds international standards.

Modern technology, state-of-the-art tools, and knowledgeable staff enable our network of laboratory services over the entire island to deliver precise diagnostic tests. Our facilities are designed to meet worldwide standards, and we have the newest technology available in every department. For immediate access to medical or surgical care, you can visit our Accident and Emergency facilities day or night.
