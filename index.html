DOCMED Hospitals

Welcome from DOCMED Hospitals! In our globally recognized care, you and your loved ones are in secure hands. Our focus is on patient safety, and we provide you with extremely skilled medical care in our state-of-the-art facilities, including tertiary and super specialist care.

You can relax knowing that our skilled and experienced medical staff is working around the clock to provide an exceptional patient experience at our hospitals in Galle, WTC, and Colombo.

Why Patients Choose Us?

Via our Centers of Excellence, DOCMED provides some of the most innovative, evidence-based clinical programs for treating complicated diseases in the nation by hiring, advising, and collaborating with the most committed, knowledgeable, and experienced healthcare professionals. We have a solid track record of providing excellent results.

Additionally, DOCMED provides the widest range of medical services, preventive healthcare, and treatment for diseases that are becoming more and more common due to lifestyle choices.
